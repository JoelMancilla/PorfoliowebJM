About Me

I am a design and technology enthusiast: User Interface and User Experience Designer with technical training in Programming.

My passion lies in innovating and sketching ideas to bring to life digital product interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and functional. I believe that the core of a great product is its people, so I constantly study their reactions and behaviors when interacting with it, transforming those observations into valuable insights. I am actively seeking to improve my skills every day, merging the creative perspective of design with the logical thinking of development to create coherent, viable, and user-centered digital solutions.

Thank you for visiting my little corner of the web. I'm excited to share more of my career with you!
